# Privacy Policy for Kio Weather

**Last Updated: November 2025**

## Introduction
Kio Weather ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Information
- We collect your device's location ONLY when you explicitly grant location permission
- Location data is used solely to provide weather forecasts for your current position
- Location data is sent directly to weather service APIs and is NOT stored on our servers

### User Preferences
- Language preference (German/English)
- Saved city locations
- These preferences are stored locally on your device using localStorage
- We do NOT have access to these stored preferences

## How We Use Your Information
- **Weather Data**: Your location is used to fetch weather information from Open-Meteo API
- **Geocoding**: City searches use Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) for geocoding services
- **No Analytics**: We do NOT use any analytics or tracking services
- **No Ads**: We do NOT display advertisements or share data with advertisers

## Data Storage
- All user preferences are stored locally on your device
- We do NOT operate any servers that store user data
- We do NOT create user accounts or profiles

## Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
1. **Open-Meteo API**: For weather data (https://open-meteo.com)
2. **Nominatim API**: For geocoding services (https://nominatim.openstreetmap.org)

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

## Data Security
- All API requests are made over secure HTTPS connections
- No personal data is transmitted to or stored on our servers
- Your saved locations remain on your device

## Children's Privacy
Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

## Your Rights
You can:
- Deny or revoke location permissions at any time through your device settings
- Clear all saved data by uninstalling the app
- Use the app without location services by manually searching for cities

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date.
